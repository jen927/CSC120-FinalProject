Living in a world full of magic, yet none flows within you. 
Born to one of the most powerful wizardy families in history, you have been burden with the curse of being magicless.
All your life, your parents, your peers, and society has looked down upon you as a failure.
The only hope your family has left in you is to become a formidable magic weapon wielder.
Now, you begin your journey to become the most powerful wielder in the land.
Will you take on this challenge?
